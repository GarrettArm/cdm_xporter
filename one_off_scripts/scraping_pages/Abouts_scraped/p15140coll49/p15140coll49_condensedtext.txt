This collection is comprised of academic catalogs and course descriptions for LSU Health Sciences Center/LSU Medical Center. Includes curricula, educational plans, offerings, descriptions, requirements and faculty rosters for various health sciences professions taught at LSU Health Sciences Center - New Orleans. Schools include: Medicine, Graduate Studies, Nursing, Dentistry, Allied Health Professions and Public Health. LSU Health Sciences Center Shreveport campus included for some years. Special thanks to:Sabrina Caston, Library Associate, John P Ische' Library - LSU Health Sciences Center, New Orleans Zerita Dupre, Library Associate, Dental Library - LSU Health Sciences Center, New Orleans This collection was created in May, 2011.
