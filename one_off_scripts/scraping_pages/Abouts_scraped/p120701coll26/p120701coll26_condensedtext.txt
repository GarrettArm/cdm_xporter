Printed graduation programs from commencement ceremonies at LSU Medical Center and LSU Health Sciences Center, New Orleans, 1933-1945, 1947-1961, 1966 to present.This collection includes names of all graduating students from the LSU campus in New Orleans, LA, including the School of Medicine, School of Dentistry, School of Nursing, School of Allied Health, School of Graduate Studies and School of Public Health. This collection was created in 2009 with the help of the LSUHSC-NO Registrar's Office.
